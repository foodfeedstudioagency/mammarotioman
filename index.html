❧ OUR STORY ❧

More Than Food — It's Home

Mamma Roti was born from a simple belief: food should taste like home. Inspired by a mother's love and the warmth of homemade cooking, we bring together rich flavours, quality ingredients, and heartfelt hospitality in every dish we serve.

As a modern cloud kitchen, we deliver street-style favourites, comforting Indian classics, and fusion dishes — all prepared with the consistency of a restaurant and the soul of a family kitchen. Every order is made fresh. Every meal, made with love.
