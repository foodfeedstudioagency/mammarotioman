Who We Are

Born from a kitchen,
built with love

Mamma Roti started with one simple belief — great food doesn't need a fancy address. From the streets of Mumbai to the heart of Muscat, every dish carries the soul of home cooking.

We're a cloud kitchen serving Muscat families, office teams and celebration tables — with fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and flavours that remind you of home.

From a quick weekday lunch to a fully catered birthday party — Mamma Roti is your table, your way. We believe food is more than fuel — it's memory, comfort, and connection.
