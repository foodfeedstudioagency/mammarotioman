Who We Are

Born from a kitchen,
built with love

Mamma Roti started with one simple belief — great food doesn't need a fancy address. From the streets of Mumbai to the heart of Muscat, every dish we make carries the soul of home cooking.

We're a cloud kitchen serving Muscat families, office teams, and celebration tables — with fresh ingredients, authentic recipes, and the kind of flavours that remind you of home.

From a quick weekday lunch to a full catered birthday party — Mamma Roti is your table, your way. Whether it's a bowl of Pani Puri on a Tuesday afternoon or Chicken Biryani for 50 guests, we bring the same love to every order.

We believe food is more than fuel — it's memory, comfort, and connection. That's what drives everything we make.
